== Description ==

TW Image Hover Share allows you to share every image on your page higher than 300px in width and 100px in height.

Just hover over the image and you'll see the TW Image Hover menu. Click on the menu to share and instantly start sharing 
images throughout the internet.

== Installation ==

Upload the TW Image Hover plugin to your blog, Activate it.

Go to settings page on your admin panel, select your social networks and layout. Do not forget to click submit.
